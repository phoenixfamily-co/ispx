From Sketch to real life The Iranian shining Phoenix Group was established with the collective effort of aviation industry experts and with the aim of making profit by using the latest technologies and innovations of the world's aviation industry. The spread of some new methods and techniques in the world's aviation industry, such as dry washing or aircraft polishing, has led to the possibility of providing new and useful services in the aviation industry. On the other hand, the increase in the need for air transportation and passenger flights, the need for optimal use of resources in the presence of sanctions, as well as the need to attention to social responsibility, comply with environmental requirements, saving water consumption and etc., require the adoption of new solutions made it inevitable.  

This company is trying to open a new market in providing services to airlines by transferring and using new but simple, cost-effective and effective methods, materials and tools in Iran's aviation. A service that, in addition to increasing the safety and interest of passengers to use flight services in their travels, also results in reducing the costs and profitability of airlines.
